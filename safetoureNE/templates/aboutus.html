About Smart Tour

SafeTour Kerala is tourist safety and travel assistant application. It helps travelers by providing real-time alerts about natural disasters, security threats, and nearby emergency services like hospitals, police stations, hotels, and restaurants. With AI-powered tracking, users stay safe and connected throughout their journey.
